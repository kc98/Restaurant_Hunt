Chemistry Cafe+Lab

❮ ❯

CHEMISTRY Cafe+Lab is the first Cafe with the Lab concept in Malaysia.

Specialized in coffee and drinks, CHEMISTRY Cafe also offers a variety of cakes and pastries that are sure to whip out your appetite. CHEMISTRY Cafe uses a unique lab concept that utilize lab apparatus instead of normal glasses and cups for customers to experience dining in lab environment. Apart from lab apparatus, CHEMISTRY Cafe have a very eccentric interior design that is surely able to let customers dine with style and comfort.
