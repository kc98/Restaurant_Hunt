Kakuni

Our signature braised pork belly is made for over 4 hours of slow simmering. It has light hints of dried chilli, star anise, cinnamon and cloves. An absolute must-try for all patrons of Sozo.

Ramen

When it comes to ramen, one cannot help but think of Sozo. Our ramen has received rave reviews from food connoisseurs and the press - Sin Chew Jit Poh, Nanyang Siang Pau. Need we say more ?

Gyoza

We pride ourselves with having one of( if not ) the best gyoza amongst Japanese restaurants. Our recipe for the filling has been carefully concocted and modified over the years to achieve the perfect balance to cater to the palate of the discerning.
