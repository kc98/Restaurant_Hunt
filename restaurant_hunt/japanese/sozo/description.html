About Sozo

Also known as Japanese Chinese cuisine or Chuka, is a style of Japanese cuisine which was popular in Japan in the late 19th Century. Chuka diskes originated in China, but have been modified over the years to cater to the Japanese Palate.

In Fact, the cuisine has changed so much that nobody really knows if it is indeed Japanese or Chinese food. The Japanese consider the cuisine as chinese though the same dish would never be made the same way in a chiense restaurant (i.e - Mabo Tofu)

At Sozo, Chuka serves as the foundation of our menu. They pride ourselves as an establishment, repopularizing this form of cuisine, which was the raze in 19th century Japan. Serving good food from out hearts has always been our motto.
