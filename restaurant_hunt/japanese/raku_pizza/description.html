About Raku Pizza

Where a Memorable Culinary Experience Awaits

Entangled in an idea of fusion inspired food, born was the idea of artisanal Japanese inspired pizzas. By using only quality ingredients and a whole lot of passion, our uniquely inspired recipes combines the complex and crunchy foundation of pizza dough with the perfect blend and mix of quality ingredients. At Raku Pizza, we believe in serving you simplicity at its best.

Combining the elements of the different culinary in our search for good food brought about the birth of our new fusion menu.
