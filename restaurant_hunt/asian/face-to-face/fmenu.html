Pan Mee with a Sarawakian Twist

Affordable to make, nutritious and deliciously filling, Pan Mee is a common household Malaysian Chinese dish. At Face to Face, they use the traditional Sarawakian method inspired by the age-old Jook-Sing technique, to make their Pan Mee, resulting in noodles that truly stand out in terms of its excellent texture, elasticity, smoothness, colour and nutrition, all without using any preservatives or alkali. Want to learn how to tell the difference? Just bite into their noodles, and experience the smooth, chewy and exquisite mouthfeel for yourself.


Signature Sarawakian Creations for an Authentic Treat

For a taste of Sarawak, you cannot go wrong with famous Sarawak Noodles, also called "kolok mee". A staple food in the state, you will find it at every corner of every street there. Now, they are bringing the original taste of Sarawak Noodles to your part of town! Savour these wholesome, chewy noodles served with minced pork and Char Siew, so simple, yet so irresistible!
